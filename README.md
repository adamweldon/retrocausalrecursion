## Methodology: Subtractive Constraint Mapping

The Resonant Recursion Framework is developed through subtractive constraint mapping: retaining only what cannot be removed without contradicting observation. Each preprint in the series represents a documented removal rather than a speculative addition. What survives is the residue — the standing wave that doesn't cancel under the pressure of empirical and logical adjacency.

The starting move is to ask, for any pattern that persists: *what must not cancel when this pattern meets itself around a closed circuit?* Rationals phase-lock and cancel. Irrationals never exactly return. Among irrationals, φ resists rational approximation most strongly, with continued fraction [1; 1, 1, 1, …]. Under adjacency pressure, φ is therefore residue, not input. Standing waves are what didn't cancel.

This subtractive method has produced a constraint-consistency discipline with four named anchors:

1. **Four-anchor frame** — Independent constraints from particle physics (Standard Model parameter space), molecular biology (DNA helical geometry), gravitational-wave astronomy (LIGO/Virgo black hole mass distributions), and cosmology (Hubble tension). Each anchor constrains the framework's interior from a different direction; all four agreeing on the same φ-harmonic operator is the load-bearing claim.
2. **Ratio cleanliness discipline** — Predicted ratios must hit observed values within experimental precision, not within hand-tunable tolerance bands. Wide tails are flagged; tight matches are pre-registered for replication.
3. **Honest parameter counting** — Free parameters are counted by what cannot be derived from prior commitments, not by what has not yet been derived. The current honest count is 4–6 (down from 22 in v1 of the audit), with remaining items classified as derivation-pending rather than free choice.
4. **Pre-registration seal** — Predictions are uploaded with timestamp before the data they predict becomes available, where the empirical situation permits. This is an epistemic discipline, not a marketing claim.

A separate Constraint-Consistency Methodology document formalizes these disciplines and is referenced by all preprints in the series.
